Dry Farming Suitability Map of Oregon

Suitable Sites for Dry Farming

Once all the climate attributes are combined with accountability made for state-owned lands, exclude, we are able to find zones that are suitable for implementing Dry Farming techniques. It is our hope that is map will allow you to assess your farmland to adjust to irrigating less, which will allow for heatwave tolerant crops to furious, and for more water to remain in the streams and creeks to maintain aquatic health.

Attributes of Dry Farming

Below are map layers that created our suitablilty anaylsis:

  • Precipitation
  • Available Water Storage
  • Frost Free Periods
