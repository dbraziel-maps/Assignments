Frost Free Periods

All zones in ORANGE display areas with suitable frost-free periods.

The Annual Frost Free Period refers to the anticipated duration, in days, between the final occurrence of freezing temperatures in spring (January to July) and the initial occurrence of freezing temperatures in fall (August to December). This calculation is based on a probability estimation that the values for the standard normal period will be surpassed in 5 out of 10 years.
