Dry Farming Suitability Map of Oregon

Precipitation

This data was collected from Oregon State University’s PRISM Climate Group database. It’s the monthly averages of annual condition over the thirty years, from 1991-2020. The long-term averages are modeled with PRISM using a digital elevation model (DEM) with seven other climate elements, such as precipitation.

We selected for areas that have 20 inches or more of precipitaion.
